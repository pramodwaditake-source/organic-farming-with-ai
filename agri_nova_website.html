About AgriNova

AgriNova AI Farms Pvt. Ltd. is a private company focused on scaling organic agriculture by embedding AI and smart tech into traditional farming. Our model blends ecological methods with data-driven decision making.

Mission

Integrate AI and IoT technologies with natural farming for maximum yield and minimum waste. Build self-reliant farming communities through training and data tools.

Objectives (1–5 years)

  • Establish 10–20 acres pilot with AI monitoring
  • Develop AgriNova App & secure organic certification
  • Scale to 100 acres; launch AI-FaaS

Products & Services

Organic Produce

Vegetables, fruits, millets, grains and herbal plants grown with biofertilizers & vermiculture.

AI Farm Management

ML models for soil health, pest prediction, and yield optimization. App alerts for farmers.

IoT & Drones

Soil sensors, automated drip irrigation and drone surveillance with thermal & multispectral imaging.

Blockchain Traceability

Full supply chain transparency from seed to shelf.

Consultancy & Training

Farmer workshops, custom farm design and subscription analytics.

Technology Licensing

License AgriNova AI to cooperatives and agritech firms.

Market & Competitive Edge

Target customers include health-conscious consumers, retailers, restaurants, and export markets. AgriNova's advantage is an integrated tech-ecosystem for certified organic farms and farmer empowerment programs.

Industry outlook

Growing organic demand and increasing AI adoption create a strong runway for expansion.

Challenges

Initial tech costs, farmer awareness, and certification processes.

Go-to-market

Pilot clusters, partnerships with universities, digital marketing, and membership programs.
